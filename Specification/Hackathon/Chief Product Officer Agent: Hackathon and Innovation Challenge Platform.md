# Chief Product Officer Agent Response

## Product Vision Statement

To empower innovators by providing a unified, AI-driven platform that streamlines the entire hackathon and innovation challenge lifecycle, from ideation to impact.

## Target Personas

| Persona | Description | Needs & Goals |
|---|---|---|
| **Event Organizer** | Individuals or teams from corporations, universities, or communities responsible for planning and executing hackathons and innovation challenges. | - Seamless event creation and management<br>- Attracting and engaging the right participants<br>- Efficiently managing submissions and judging<br>- Demonstrating the ROI of their events |
| **Participant** | Developers, designers, students, and professionals looking to learn, build, and network. | - Finding relevant and exciting events<br>- Forming effective teams<br>- Accessing resources and mentorship<br>- Showcasing their skills and projects |
| **Judge/Mentor** | Industry experts, faculty, or experienced professionals who evaluate projects and guide participants. | - Clear and consistent judging criteria<br>- Tools to efficiently review and score submissions<br>- Contextual information about projects and teams<br>- Opportunities to connect with and mentor talent |
| **Enterprise Sponsor** | Companies that sponsor events to drive innovation, recruit talent, and promote their brand and technologies. | - Reaching a targeted audience of developers and innovators<br>- Sourcing innovative solutions to business challenges<br>- Identifying and recruiting top talent<br>- Measuring the impact and ROI of their sponsorship |

## Problem Statements

- **Event Organizers** struggle with the fragmented and manual processes of creating and managing events, leading to inefficiencies and a suboptimal experience for all stakeholders.
- **Participants** find it challenging to discover relevant events, form effective teams, and get the support they need to succeed, limiting their ability to learn and innovate.
- **Judges and Mentors** are often overwhelmed by the volume of submissions and lack the tools to provide fair and insightful feedback, diminishing the value of their contribution.
- **Enterprise Sponsors** have difficulty measuring the ROI of their event sponsorships and struggle to connect with the most relevant talent and innovations.

## MVP Feature List

- **AI Event Creation Assistant:** Basic event page generation with customizable templates for rules, schedules, and branding.
- **AI Team Formation:** Skill-based matching and team-building recommendations.
- **Project Submission and Judging:** A streamlined workflow for submitting projects and a structured judging process with basic scoring rubrics.
- **AI Search and Discovery:** Semantic search for events and participants.

## Phased Roadmap

| Phase | Timeline | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| **MVP** | 3 Months | - AI Event Creation Assistant (Basic)<br>- AI Team Formation (Basic)<br>- Project Submission & Judging<br>- AI Search & Discovery (Events & Participants) |

## Success Metrics (KPIs)

- **Platform Growth:**<br>  - Number of active users (organizers, participants, judges, sponsors)<br>  - Number of events created and completed
- **Engagement:**<br>  - Participant application and acceptance rate<br>  - Team formation rate<br>  - Project submission rate
- **Satisfaction:**<br>  - Net Promoter Score (NPS) from all user personas<br>  - User retention and churn rates
- **Business:**<br>  - Revenue from enterprise subscriptions and event fees<br>  - Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and Lifetime Value (LTV)
