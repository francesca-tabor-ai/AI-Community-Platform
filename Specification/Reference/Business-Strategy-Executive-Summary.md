# Business Strategy — Executive Summary

> Quick reference for the Creator Publishing Platform. Full strategy: [`Specification/Community/CEO Agent: Business Strategy for a Creator Publishing Platform.md`](../Community/CEO%20Agent%3A%20Business%20Strategy%20for%20a%20Creator%20Publishing%20Platform.md)

## Core Model

**Creator-Centric Subscription Marketplace** — Connects creators (supply) with readers (demand). Primary revenue: recurring subscriptions. Platform scales via network effects.

## Revenue Model

| Stream | Mechanism | Status |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Creator Revenue Share** | 10–20% of subscription revenue | Primary |
| **Premium Creator Tools** | Advanced analytics, support, branding | Future |
| **Transaction Fees** | Tips, gifting | Future |

## Pricing

- **Creators:** No upfront cost; revenue share only when they earn
- **Readers:** Creator-set tier pricing (basic, premium, VIP)
- **Freemium:** Free + paid content to drive conversions

## GTM Phases

1. **Creator Acquisition** — Direct outreach, partnerships, content marketing, referral program
2. **Audience Growth** — SEO, social sharing, discovery, email
3. **Ecosystem Expansion** — Integrations, API, community features

## Sustainability Principles

- Product-led growth
- Creator retention focus
- Data-driven decisions
- Controlled scaling & cost management
