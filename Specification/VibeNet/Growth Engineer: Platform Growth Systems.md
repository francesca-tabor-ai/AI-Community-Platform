## Platform: VibeNet — AI-Powered Professional Networking

## Growth Feature Ideas

### Acquisition Features

**Viral Referral Programme:**
- "Invite a colleague" — both parties get 30 days Professional tier free
- Unique referral link per user, tracked via UTM parameters
- Target: 30% of new users from referrals by Month 6

**SEO-Optimised Public Profiles:**
- Public profile pages indexed by search engines
- Structured data markup (Person schema) for rich search results
- Estimated 15% of new signups from organic search by Month 12

**LinkedIn Import:**
- One-click profile import from LinkedIn (OAuth)
- Reduces onboarding friction; increases profile completeness from day 1
- Estimated 40% increase in activation rate

### Retention Features

**Weekly AI Network Digest:**
- Personalised email every Monday: top 3 matches, top 3 opportunities
- AI-generated summary of network activity
- Estimated 25% increase in weekly active users

**Profile Strength Score:**
- Visible score (0–100) with specific improvement suggestions
- "Your match quality will increase by 12% if you add your GitHub profile"
- Gamification: badges for profile milestones

**Milestone Celebrations:**
- In-app celebration on: first connection, 10th, 100th connection
- Shareable achievement cards for social media
- Estimated 8% of users share milestones (organic acquisition)

### Engagement Features

**AI Conversation Starters:**
- When a new connection is made, AI generates 3 personalised conversation openers
- Based on shared skills, mutual connections, and recent activity
- Estimated 40% increase in connection-to-conversation conversion

**"People You May Know" Widget:**
- Appears on every page (sidebar widget)
- 3 suggestions, refreshed daily

## Experiment Design

### Experiment 1: Onboarding Match Reveal
- **Hypothesis:** Showing matches before profile completion increases 7-day activation by 20%
- **Metric:** 7-day activation rate (sent ≥1 connection request)
- **Sample size:** 5,000 users per variant | **Duration:** 4 weeks

### Experiment 2: Match Explanation Depth
- **Hypothesis:** 3-sentence AI explanation increases match acceptance rate by 15%
- **Metric:** Match acceptance rate within 48 hours
- **Sample size:** 10,000 match impressions per variant | **Duration:** 2 weeks

### Experiment 3: Weekly Digest Personalisation
- **Hypothesis:** AI-personalised subject lines increase open rate by 30%
- **Metric:** Email open rate, click-through rate, 7-day re-engagement
- **Sample size:** 20,000 users per variant | **Duration:** 4 weeks
